Upcoming Home Design Trends For 2023

Upcoming Home Design Trends For 2023

  • Posted On24.12.2022
  • ByDavid Thairu

What home design trends will be having a major moment in 2023? While it is exciting to see what lies ahead, it turns out that more people are looking after home design services geared towards a sense of style, elegance and a sophisticated look.

As far as fostering is concerned, the upcoming home deal trends of 2023, will only serve to refine and strengthen what the design world adores right now.

So, what kind of home design trends will make a splash in 2023? Keep reading to catch a glimpse of what lies ahead.

Handcrafted Sourcing

People prefer filling their homes with original items that are raw and refined. They are also looking for ways to support their local communitarian practices – from commissioning custom dining tables and furniture made from local wood to renovating bathrooms and kitchens with handmade tiles.

The trend can work in people's homes in many ways, and in 2023, there will be a heightened interest in hand and locally-produced home décor. As a result, handmade accessories will make decor and furniture more desirable and trendy because they will leave a unique and incomparable effect.

Art Décor

Revisiting past home design trends has become a favorite activity for many. As such, home designers are also moving away from the absoluteness of minimalism. Even though hyper maximalism is yet to occur, there is a renewed interest and a resurgence in the art deco design. People anticipate seeing lots of fun powder baths and gathering areas with the impression of art deco.

In the words of Tatiana Sieklay- if you don't wish to commit to something bigger and permanent, consider adding the touches of art décor to your accessories.

Designer Laundry and Mudroom

2023's home design trends will certainly grace all interior corners. There will be a heightened focus by homemakers on creating homes that will serve the occupants. Every room in the home will be given the designer treatment, including the laundry and mushroom rooms.

Decorative Door Glass

More homeowners are installing decorative glass doors to make their spaces feel more open. The presence of glass doors creates a welcoming atmosphere in homes and allows for plenty of sunlight, which boosts energy levels.

Unlike solid doors, patterned glass doors offer a sleek, elegant look that complements a home's front entry way. From a safety and an aesthetic point of view, more people will continue to replace solid doors with decorative glass doors come 2023 and beyond.

Below are some of the different door glasses that are becoming popular

  • Aluminum glass door – It blends perfectly with the industrial-style decor
  • Arched door design – it has sleek framing and partial view to maintain privacy
  • Glass patio door – essential for maintaining temperature in your home
  • Sliding glass door – the most common glass door due to its aesthetic appeal and space-saving
  • French glass door – contains a single hinged door that opens away from each other
  • Frameless glass door – has a sleek design to make it look extravagant
  • Modern 3D glass door design – these doors have been heightened with 3D graphics for an unearthly appeal
